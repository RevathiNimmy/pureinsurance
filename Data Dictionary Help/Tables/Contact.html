Contact

All contacts (telephone, fax, email, etc.) used by Parties. They are stored independently of the Party so that they can be shared. For example: several clients having the same email address.

For corporate clients, if there is a main contact person, they are stored as a contact record with a contact_type_id of Contact_Type(code = 'MAIN').

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure Core Back-Office
Software module: Party
