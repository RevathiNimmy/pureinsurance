Party_Lifestyle

Holds extra data for Parties of type "PC" (Personal Client). Although this table appears to hold multiple rows per Party, in fact it is expected to behave just like a one-to-one subtable. It is mandatory to have one row in for each personal client. Other types of Party, e.g. Drivers, Witnesses, etc. may optionally have data defined in here. Also used to capture personal details of family members of a Party.
