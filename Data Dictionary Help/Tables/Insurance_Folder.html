Insurance_Folder

Main policy header table, containing just those items that do not change throughout the lifetime of the policy or whose changes do not need to be tracked. As a result, most policy data is stored in Insurance_File.

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure Core Back-Office
Software module: Policy
