GIS_Insurer

Insurer who provides a scheme. Each row corresponds to a Party of type "Insurer". The link to the Party is currently deduced in one of three ways depending on the software: (a) gis_insurer_id = Party.party_cnt, (b) by polaris_insurer_no, and (c) abi_81_insurer = Party.abi_code_on_81.

Logical database: GIS
Component team: Pure Architecture
Software module: GIS
