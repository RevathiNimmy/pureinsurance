Credit_Control_Item

This holds an individual amount that is being (or going to be) chased for by the Credit Contol system. Each record will either point to a total debt against an insurance policy or an individual instalment on a Finance Plan.

Logical database: Orion
Component team: Pure Accounts
Software module: Accounts
