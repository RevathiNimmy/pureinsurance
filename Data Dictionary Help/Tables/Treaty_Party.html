Treaty_Party

Links reinsurers to a treaty, and includes what share of the treaty's premium they have.

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure For Underwriting
Software module: Risk

Definition

treaty_id integer not null FK ð Treaty.treaty_id links to the treaty
share_percent float not null   what share of the treaty's premium does this party have (adds up to 100 across all records against a treaty)
commission_percent float not null   What's the commission rate in this treaty arrangement
