PFRF

Rates Files. Each record is valid between Start and End Date and for a particular Product Family. The rate applied will be for amounts falling between 1 and 5 Ranges (Min1-5, Max1-5).

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure Accounts
Software module: Instalments
