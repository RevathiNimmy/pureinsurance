Contact_Address_Usage

Links Contacts to Addresses. Houses and business premises might have multiple telephone, fax and email addresses associated with the building, not the people within.
Home phone number of corporate clients is stored as a contact of their correspondence address type. Work phone number of personal clients is stored as a contact of their business address type.

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure Core Back-Office
Software module: Party
