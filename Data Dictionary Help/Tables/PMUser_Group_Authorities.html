PMUser_Group_Authorities

This is used by the enhanced Orion security product option to assign view (enquiry) or update rights for a PMUser_Group to a folder within the Structure tree. Folder rights do not cascade down and, by default, there are no rights for any groups.

Logical database: Orion
Component team: Pure Accounts
Software module: Accounts
