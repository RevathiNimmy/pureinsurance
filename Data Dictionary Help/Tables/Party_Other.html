Party_Other

Holds extra data for Parties of types "OTDRIVER", "OTWITNESS", "OTREPAIRER", "OTTHIRD" (all parties involved in a road accident). Used mostly in Claims and Product Builder. New party types can be defined and used with this table as long as their codes begin with "OT".

Logical database: BackOffice
Component team: Pure Core Back-Office
Software modules: Claims, Party, Product Builder
