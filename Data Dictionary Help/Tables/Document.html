Document

Documents group Transactions (TransDetail records) together identifying when they were posted into Orion. They are the general ledger headers for Account Receivable (debit) and Account Receivable Payable (credit) transactions. All TransDetail records within a Document will add up to zero.

Logical database: Orion
Component team: Pure Accounts
Software module: Accounts
